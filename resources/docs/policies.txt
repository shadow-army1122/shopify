Commission Fees:
- The standard commission rate is 10% on the final sale price of each item.
- For electronics, the commission rate is reduced to 8%.
- Commission is deducted automatically before the payout is processed to your linked bank account.

Payout Schedule:
- Payouts are processed weekly on Mondays.
- You must have a minimum balance of $50 to be eligible for a payout.
- Please ensure your banking details are up-to-date in the Settings tab to avoid delays.

Shipping Policy:
- Sellers are responsible for shipping items within 3 business days of an order being placed.
- You must provide a valid tracking number for every order.
- Failure to ship on time may result in temporary suspension of your seller account.
- We recommend using our partnered carriers for discounted rates and automatic tracking updates.

Returns & Refunds:
- Buyers have a 14-day window to request a return for defective or significantly not-as-described items.
- If a return is approved, you must provide a return shipping label.
- Refunds are processed once the item is received back and inspected.
- "Change of mind" returns are at the seller's discretion unless otherwise stated in your shop policy.

Prohibited Items:
- The following items are strictly prohibited: illegal substances, weapons, counterfeit goods, and hazardous materials.
- Listing prohibited items will result in immediate permanent ban.

Account Health:
- Maintain an order defect rate below 1% to keep your Top Seller status.
- Respond to buyer messages within 24 hours to ensure high customer satisfaction.
